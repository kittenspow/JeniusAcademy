Frequently Asked Question (FAQ)

What age groups and education levels does JeniusAcademy cater to?
JeniusAcademy is designed for students ranging from elementary school to those preparing for college entrance exams. Our platform offers tailored content and resources to meet the needs of diverse age groups and education levels.
What are the differences between the subscription packages?
JeniusAcademy offers five subscription packages: Basic, Smart, Genius, Lucky, and Supernova. Each package builds on the previous one, offering additional benefits such as interactive learning tools, personalized study plans, one-on-one tutoring sessions, advanced progress tracking, and exclusive access to premium content and support services.
How can I schedule one-on-one tutoring sessions?
One-on-one tutoring sessions can be scheduled through your JeniusAcademy account dashboard. Depending on your subscription package, you will have a set number of sessions available each month. Simply choose a time that works for you and connect with a tutor directly through our platform.
Can I upgrade my subscription package at any time?
Yes, you can upgrade your subscription package at any time through your account settings. Upgrading allows you to immediately access the additional features and benefits of the higher-tier package.
How does the progress tracker work?
Our progress tracker helps you monitor your academic performance and improvement over time. It provides detailed analytics on your quiz and exercise results, tracks your study hours, and offers insights into areas where you can improve.
